Seeker FAQ (For Kimberley Community & Students)

Who is eligible to apply for assistance?

Currently, we assist registered full-time students at accredited institutions and Kimberley community members who can demonstrate financial need.

How do I apply for food, clothing, or sanitary parcels?

Simply click the "Apply Now" button on our homepage. You will need to upload a copy of your ID or student card to verify your status.

How long does the approval process take?

Applications are usually processed within 48 hours. Once approved, you will receive a collection code via SMS.

Is my personal information kept private?

Yes. Your financial status and personal details are strictly confidential; we only share anonymized statistics (e.g., "A family of four" or "A 2nd-year student") with our donors.

What should I bring for collection?

Please bring your physical ID or Student Card to the designated venue at the date and time specified in your confirmation.

Where can I find the distribution points?

We have an interactive Google Map embedded on our "Contact Information" page to help you find our physical office or drop-off centers easily.
